# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free collaboration environment.

## Expected behavior
- Be constructive and precise.
- Focus on code and technical decisions.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks or deliberate disruption.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate this policy.
